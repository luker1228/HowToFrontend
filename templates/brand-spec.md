## Observed posture

1. Use a paper-like warm light background instead of pure white.
2. Headlines are heavy, condensed, uppercase-leaning, and navy-black rather than true black.
3. Cards have clear 1.5px to 2px borders, small radii, and almost no shadow.
4. Accent budget stays tight: orange-red for CTA and key emphasis, yellow for small labels only.
5. Layout favors dense rows of bordered modules with generous outer whitespace and compact inner spacing.
